Camping

Prefering summer and Fall, I have an extensive record of camping all year long. As a boy this love of nature was nourished by some of the scouting activities and camping trips that we took. As I continue to camp, my appreciation for nature grows, and my desire for a warmer, comfier bed also increases.

Hiking

From natural canyons, to mountain peaks, to lava tubes, I'll never turn down a great hike, especially if there are excellent views. There's nothing like the rush of endorphines from a good workout to concrete a lovely view of nature into your mind. There's no reward quite like it, chemically speaking.

Conservational Planting

Planting local species of flowers and other pollinating plants helps me to care for the bees and other pollinating fauna that support our ecosystem. While I cannot make many other contributions, this method of giving back to mother nature fills me with a warming sense of connectivity to this planet that's sustained my life for almost 3 decades now.

Cleanup Efforts

Taking special care to pick up garbage and other human-made objects that litter beaches, hiking trails, camping grounds, and other locations that I visit helps me to feel proud of my surroundings, my impact on them, and aids in the preservation of those environments for others after me. I aim to complete at least 2 cleanup projects a year. Some are larger than others, admittedly, but it's a passion that I maintain.
